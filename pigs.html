Heritage Gloucestershire Old Spots Pigs

  • Registered breeding stock and feeder pigs
  • Selected Boars by Reservation Only
  • Barrows available
  • Low coefficient of inbreeding

Our Gloucestershire Old Spots (GOS) pigs graze on organic pasture and eat organic, non-GMO grains. We do not feed them any antibiotics, hormones, or "spent" crops that are the waste produce from breweries or food processing plants.

Our GOS pigs are healthy and happy and live in a stress-free environment where disease is not an issue.

The GOS heritage breed pig is excellent on pasture. Raising these pigs on pasture has many benefits for the consumer, the most important benefit being that the meat has a higher nutrient density than pork from pigs raised only on grains and on a cement slab. Unfortunately, these pigs are on the Livestock Conservancy's critical list.

The Livestock Conservancy has more information about GOS pigs.
