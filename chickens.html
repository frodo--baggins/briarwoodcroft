We recieve our day-old chicks directly from the hatchery. Our chickens are fed organic feed with no antibiotics or other chemicals from the time the arrive here on.

We have available for sale brown large and extra-large eggs. We also raise Ameraucana hens that lay turquoise to green eggs.

All our hens are free to roam our organic pasture and eat grass, bugs, worms, and other insects that have been the diet of chickens for hundreds of years. Our eggs have beautiful orange yolks that are produced from roaming free on pasture grasses.
