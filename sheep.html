Our Corriedale sheep are dual-purpose sheep, providing wool and meat. The corriedale is a large-horned sheep that produces good quality meat. We sell male and female lambs. Intact males available by ordering ahead. We also can provide lambs that have long tails which are not docked.

The Corriedale is he oldest of all the crossbred breeds and was first brought to the United States in 1914. Corriedales adapt well to a wide range of climate conditions and are hardy in winter climates. They are docile sheep who are good mothers. Corriedales produce a thick, stapled, bulky fleece which is popular with spinners and can be used for a range of handspun garments.
